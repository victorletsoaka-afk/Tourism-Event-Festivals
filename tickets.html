Register for the Festival

Tickets are available for visitors who wish to attend the festival and participate in the different activities offered during the event. The ticket prices are designed to be affordable so that both local residents and international tourists can attend and enjoy the celebration. Visitors are encouraged to register early to secure their participation in the event. Registration helps the organizers prepare for the number of guests and ensure that everyone enjoys a well-organized and memorable festival experience.
