Event Overview

The Maletsunyane Cultural and Adventure Festival is one of the most exciting tourism events in Lesotho. It celebrates Basotho culture, music, traditional food and adventure tourism.

Tourists from around the world visit Semonkong to enjoy cultural dances, music performances, and the beauty of the famous Maletsunyane Falls.
