Contact Lesotho Tourism
Visitors are encouraged to experience the unique beauty and culture of Lesotho by attending the Maletsunyane Cultural and Adventure Festival. The event provides an excellent opportunity to explore one of the country’s most remarkable natural attractions while enjoying traditional Basotho music, dance, and local cuisine. Tourists will also have the chance to participate in exciting outdoor activities such as hiking, pony trekking, and viewing the magnificent Maletsunyane Falls. The festival creates a welcoming environment for both local and international visitors who wish to learn more about Basotho culture and heritage. For more information about the event, visitors are encouraged to contact the tourism team through the email, phone number, or social media platforms provided above. We look forward to welcoming you to an unforgettable cultural and adventure experience in Lesotho.
