Festival Schedule

The festival offers a wide range of activities that are designed to entertain and educate visitors. Cultural performances such as traditional Basotho music and dance are among the highlights of the event. Visitors can also explore exhibitions of local arts and crafts created by talented artisans from different parts of the country. Adventure activities are also a major attraction during the festival. Tourists can participate in hiking, pony trekking, sightseeing, and the famous Maletsunyane abseiling experience. These activities allow visitors to enjoy the natural landscape while experiencing thrilling outdoor adventures.
