History and Significance

The Maletsunyane Cultural Festival was established to promote tourism in Lesotho and celebrate the culture and traditions of the Basotho people.

The event brings together artists, musicians and cultural performers from across the country to showcase traditional Basotho heritage.

Target Tourists

  1. Adventure tourists
  2. Cultural tourists
  3. International visitors
  4. Local travelers
